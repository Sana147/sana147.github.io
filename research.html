research

My research falls under the category of Network Security and Android Systems Security.

In Network Security, I study the existing vulnerabilities in SDN architecture to improve SDN security by developing SDN defenses and a vulnerability-resilience rating framework for SDN to give a reliable backbone to this critical technology.

In Android Systems Security, I mainly reverse engineer apps to look for vulnerabilities in the network infrastructure itself. For this purpose, I study Android apps for targeted attacks. These attacks are for at-risk populations (such as journalists and activists).

My list of peer-reviewed publications is on Google Scholar.
